# Teams

## GET teams for an event (league+season)

- `/event/:key/teams`

Examples:

- `/event/world.2014/teams`  -- World Cup Teams (Brazil 2014)
- `/event/de.2013_14/teams`  -- Deutsche Bundesliga Teams (Season 2013/14)
